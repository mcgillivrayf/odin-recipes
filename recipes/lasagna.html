Lasagna: a standout in the realm casseroles

(yours will probably not look this good)

Welcome!

If you hate Mondays as much as I do, then you know there's only one thing that can help you get over those beginning-of-the-week blues: a heaping, steaming, sloppy pile of lasagna, of course! Let's get right into it.

For this recipe, you will need the following:

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomato sauce
  • some meat for the sauce
  • onion, garlic, and a whole lotta luck
  • spices, seasonings, and everything nice
  • cheese (whatever kind you can find in your fridge)
  • an egg.
  • the will to go on

Here are the steps:

  1. heat up your oven ("pretty hot" should do just fine)
  2. cook the noodles and then add them into a pan
  3. add the other stuff in with the noodles
  4. put the whole thing in the oven
  5. take it out when it's done and then eat it if you want
  6. subscribe to my cooking blog

Congrats! If you managed to follow these simple steps, you now have a complete lasagna ready to devour! Load up your trough and chow down like the little piggy you are!
